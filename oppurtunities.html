The following projects are available in Dr. Alaie’s laboratory at the department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. Students who are interested to gain hands-on or theoretical experience in the areas of microfabrication, microsystems, medical implants, artificial intelligence and advanced manufacturing are encouraged to contact .

- Fabrication of miniaturized pneumatic actuator with aid of laser, including the following milestones:

  1. Setting up laser systems specialized for fabrication of film-like actuators.
  2. Interfacing 2D layout desin software with laser the routing systems.     
  3. Lamination of polymeric polymeric and metalic thin films.
  4. Setting up systems for 2D die cutting  and tailored fiber placement.
  5. Fabrication of ultrathin wearable pneumatic actuators.

- Microfabrication of microsystems on Si wafer and PDMS icnluding the following milestones:

  1. Design of 2D layouts for photomasks
  2. Fabrication of 2D photomask using wet etch
  3. Fabrication of a novel I-line exposure system using laser diodes  and CNC routers
  4. Exposuresystem for the photoresists
  5. Replicating the micropatterns using soft lithography techniques

-Design of advanced materials with the aid of artificial intelligence, including the following milestones:

  1. Development and integration of multipg simulation techniques
  2. Debugging python and improving code written
  3. Use of a remote workspace in simulation and storage of meta-materials
  4. Improving end-to-end ML models developed in TensorFlow
  5. Developing  genetic algorithm code in python for optimal designs
  6. Fabrication and test of the designs achieved by Artificial Intelligence
