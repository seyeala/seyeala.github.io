Seyedhamidreza Alaie received his B.S. and M. S. degrees in Mechanical Engineering from University of Tehran and Tarbiat Modares University in 2004 and 2007. He began his carrier in University of New Mexico in 2009. Currently he is a Researcher/Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering and M.S. student in Optical Science and Engineering in University of New Mexico and he is expected to graduate in 2014.

His current research interests are mainly focused on:


● Fabrication and Characterization of Phononic Crystals.
● Characterization of Different Material Properties at Nano Scale Using MEMS/NEMS.
● Thermal Electrical Characterization of Thin Films and Nano Fibers.
● Micro Opto-Mechanical Oscillators.
